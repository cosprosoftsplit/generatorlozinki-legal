Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate a password?

Open the App, choose your preferred length and character rules, then tap Generate. Premium users can also switch to pronounceable passwords and passphrases.

What does Premium unlock?

Premium removes ads and unlocks advanced generation modes, bulk generation, and full password history. It is a one-time purchase, not a subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Settings in the App and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is my password sent to your servers?

No. Password generation happens locally on your device, and password history stays on your device.

What does the widget do?

The widget can generate a fresh password using your saved settings. By default it copies the password without showing the full value on your Home Screen, but you can change that behavior in Settings.

How do I clear copied passwords from the clipboard?

You can enable clipboard auto-clear in Settings and choose how long the password should remain on the clipboard before the App clears it.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's instructions.
