Privacy Policy

Generator Lozinki - Last updated: April 9, 2026

Introduction

SoftSplit d.o.o. ("we", "us", or "our"), based in Split, Croatia, operates the Generator Lozinki mobile application ("the App"). This policy explains what data is processed when you use the App.

Passwords and History

The App generates passwords locally on your device. Generated passwords are not sent to our servers because we do not operate a password backend for this App.

If you use password history, your history is stored locally on your device in secure system storage. We do not receive, inspect, or back up your passwords.

Widget Data

If you use the home screen widget, the App stores a local widget snapshot and your widget preferences in an Apple App Group container shared between the App and the widget extension. This data stays on your device.

Clipboard

When you tap copy or use the widget's generate-and-copy action, the selected password is copied to your device clipboard. Depending on your chosen settings, the App may clear the clipboard automatically after a delay.

Advertising and Tracking

The free version of the App may display ads using Google AdMob. If you allow tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt, AdMob may use your device advertising identifier to personalize ads.

  • We do not build user profiles from your passwords.
  • We do not transmit password history to advertising providers.
  • Advertising and related identifiers are handled by third-party advertising SDKs, subject to their own policies.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional premium purchase through Apple's StoreKit framework. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive your credit card information or Apple ID credentials.

Data We Do Not Collect Ourselves

  • We do not run analytics servers for this App.
  • We do not require account creation.
  • We do not upload generated passwords or password history to our servers.

Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple services for in-app purchases and Google AdMob for advertising in the free tier. Please review the privacy policies of Apple and Google for more information about how they process data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed specifically to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through our own servers.

Your Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you may have rights under the GDPR. Since most App data stays on your device, the fastest way to delete it is to clear history inside the App or delete the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
